alternative rock blasted into low-earth orbit.

sharing an affinity for the heavy, slow + fuzzed out riffs of mid 90s rock bands, honeymoon strives to create a space for honest alternative rock music where you aren’t afraid to ask “wouldn’t this be cool if it was louder?”
